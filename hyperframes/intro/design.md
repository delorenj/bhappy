# BHappy Brand — design.md

Distilled from `.stitch/DESIGN.md`. Strict on colors, fonts, and Don'ts. Adapt scale for video.

## Palette

- Canvas Cream: `#FFF6E8` — primary background
- Canvas Deep: `#FBE9CF` — secondary background tint
- Peach: `#FFE0B8` — soft surface
- Peach Deep: `#FFC78A` — surface variant
- Ink: `#15172E` — primary text, deep navy ground (NEVER use pure black)
- Ink Soft: `#2B2F50` — body text variant
- Ink Muted: `#5A6188` — tertiary text
- Amber: `#F59410` — primary accent (Tier 01, primary CTA)
- Amber Soft: `#FFB04E` — accent highlight
- Amber Deep: `#D87208` — accent shadow side
- Coral: `#FF8A6B` — Tier 02 accent
- Rose: `#EF5B4A` — Tier 03 accent
- Plum: `#6E3FCF` — Tier 04 accent

## Typography

- Display: `Outfit`, weights 700–900, letter-spacing `-0.035em` at large sizes
- Body: `Outfit`, weight 400–600
- Mono: `JetBrains Mono`, weight 500, letter-spacing `0.18em` for eyebrows

## Corners

- Cards / pills: full radius (1.75rem to pill)
- Tiles: 1.25rem

## Mood

Warm sunshine at 4pm. Confident, not childish. Asymmetric, not cheesy. Premium-warm not Comic-Sans-warm.

## Don'ts

- No emojis. Anywhere.
- No `Inter` font.
- No pure black (`#000000`).
- No outer-glow / neon shadows.
- No fabricated metrics or fake testimonials.
- No "John Doe" / "Acme" placeholder names.
- No filler copy ("Scroll to explore", "Swipe down").
- No AI clichés ("Elevate", "Seamless", "Unleash").
- No 3-equal-card horizontal feature rows.
- No centered hero with only one focal point.

## Tier Identity

- Tier 01 Orient — Amber `#F59410`
- Tier 02 Operate — Coral `#FF8A6B`
- Tier 03 Extend — Rose `#EF5B4A`
- Tier 04 Author — Plum `#6E3FCF`
